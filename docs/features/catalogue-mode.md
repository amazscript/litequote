# Catalogue Mode

Transform your entire WooCommerce shop into a quote-only showcase. No prices, no cart, no checkout -- just quote buttons on every product.

## Enable

1. Go to **WooCommerce > LiteQuote > General**
2. Check **"Enable catalogue mode for the entire shop"**
3. Save

## What It Does

When enabled:

- **All prices** are hidden on every product (shop, category, product pages)
- **All "Add to Cart" buttons** are replaced with the LiteQuote quote button
- **Mini-cart widget** is hidden (if no products are excluded)
- **Cart page** shows a message: "This shop works on a quote basis"
- An **admin notice** reminds you that catalogue mode is active

## Exclusions

You can exclude specific products or categories from catalogue mode, allowing them to keep their normal price and purchase button.

### Exclude by Product ID

Enter comma-separated product IDs in the **"Exclude Product IDs"** field:

```
42, 108, 256
```

### Exclude by Category

Check the categories you want to exclude in the **"Exclude Categories"** section.

## Use Cases

- **B2B wholesale shops** -- all prices are negotiable
- **Custom/bespoke products** -- furniture, jewelry, custom clothing
- **Service providers** -- consulting, design, development
- **Coming soon shops** -- show your catalogue before setting prices
- **Trade shows** -- display products without public pricing

## Compatibility

Catalogue mode works with all WooCommerce product types:

- Simple products
- Variable products (variation selector remains visible)
- Grouped products
- External/affiliate products (external button is replaced)
